Holographic Appearance:

Holo stickers have a shimmering, holographic effect that makes them stand out on your weapon skin. This effect can add an element of rarity and uniqueness to the skin.

Rarity:

Holo stickers are considered more rare and desirable than regular stickers due to their distinctive appearance. They are often associated with special events, major tournaments, or limited-time offers, making them sought after by collectors and players.

Trading and Collecting:

Due to their rarity and popularity, holo stickers are often used in trading within the CS:GO community. Some players collect rare and valuable stickers as a form of in-game investment.
